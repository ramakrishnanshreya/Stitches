The idea

A music diary built from lyric patches.

Stitches is designed for moments when a lyric says the thing better than a status update can. Users save short lyric fragments as patches, then arrange multiple patches into a stitch that captures a feeling, memory, relationship, or phase.

The product combines music discovery with personal expression. Instead of endless posts, the interface focuses on a small set of meaningful actions: collect, order, stitch, like, comment, and share.
